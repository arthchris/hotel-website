Discover more about us

Located in a beautiful serene area in the Eastern Regional Capital Koforidua, Capital View Hotel has all the comfort and luxury you need for a perfect stay away from home. From arrival to departure, our friendly staff look forward to pamper you and make your stay with us a memorable one. Our State of the Art conference halls, ultra-modern decorated guest rooms, swimming pool, gym restaurant and bar are just icing on the cake to your relaxation in our Hotel.
